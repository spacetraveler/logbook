0.0.0.4 - BootStep Experiments

Jul 15th, 2015 10:30 am

Earlier I introduced BootSteps as the visual element that SpaceTraveler leaves behind. So far it only lived in a digital environment. In order to change that I explored a way to create a physical prototype. Obviously, starting with a digital mock-up yet again.

Create a blind, it will be used to shape the silicone mold.
Always use a sharp scalpel when cutting stuff.

Combine the blue with the white dough in equal shares. Do not eat.
Knead until the color of the material is even. You have five minutes to bring it into your desired shape, then the silicone mold will stay as is.

I decided on Ruby red cask resin for the final imprint. Again, do not eat. Protective gloves are furthermore advisable.

Mix two parts resin to one part of hardener.
Stir with a wooden stick.

Wait for eventual bubbles of trapped air to travel to the surface.
Pour the mixture into your silicone mold. All done.

Patiently wait for 24h.
Voila. The hardened resin is ready to be released from the mold.

While the finished piece features a fine shiny surface, the opposite side does not fulfill my expectations. It is rough and matt. Very DIY.
Polishing might improve its appearance, but for now I will resort to good old photo filters. Digital magic.
